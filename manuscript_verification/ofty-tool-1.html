Does not replace clinical judgment or tonometric assessment. Thin CCT is an independent risk factor for glaucoma development (OHTS) — eyes with extreme CCT (<480 µm or >620 µm) may require additional caution.

Linear correction following Ehlers et al. (Acta Ophthalmol, 1975): IOP adjusted = IOP measured − (CCT − 540) / 25, ref. 540 µm, 1 mmHg per 25 µm deviation.

Individual corneal biomechanics — including hysteresis, rigidity, and structural properties — are not accounted for by this model. This value does not represent true intraocular pressure.

The clinician retains full responsibility for all diagnostic and therapeutic decisions. Decision-support aid only — not a substitute for clinical evaluation. Not intended for patient self-use.
